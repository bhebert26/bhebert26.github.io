THE GEAR

MY GEAR

I first started guitar when I got into college around 2014 (Yeah, I know, very unique). I had an acoustic guitar that we bought from a friend, and then a used Wine Red Epiphone Les Paul Special II that we picked up from Guitar Center for $50. I didn't play much, mainly just learning those easy riffs that everyone can recognize (Smoke on the Water, Seven Nation Army, etc etc). It wasn't until about 2016 that I seriously started to try and learn things about actually playing the guitar.

After that, I had acquired a passion for music that went past listening. I got GAS (gear acquisition syndrome) and slowly increased my repitoire of instruments from an acoustic and an electric to a whole slew of things from electric guitars to basses, to pedals, and even electronic keyboards and drums. The journey came to an end and I eventually started playing less and less, and sold most of my gear (although I still occassionaly dabble in fun little song projects).

For what I currently have:

  • Squire J Mascis Jazzmaster (6-string Electric Guitar)
  • GTRS S801 (6-string 'Intelligent' Electric Guitar; basically has a built in fx processor)
  • Ibanez AEG2011-VV (6-string Acoustic-Electric)
